PumpkinCatz

is a collection of unique collectable cat portraits, launching soon on Cardano. It is dedicated to my beloved cat and best friend – Pumpkin.

See my work

About

There are more than 1 000 000 possible PumpkinCatz variations, with just the cutest 2 222 PumpkinCatz in the collection.
Every single PumpkinCat can only be minted once, some will happen to have very rare traits – like the pumpkin hat. Our ambition is to build an active community across all relevant CNFT platforms and bring attention to the Cardano Ecosystem.
But also to do something good - 10 % of the revenue collected will be donated to help shelterless cats.

FAQ

When will I be able to mint PumpkinCatz NFT?
TBA

What's the initial mint price?
18 ₳DA

When will I get my PumpkinCat?
After you send the funds to the wallet, it will be minted and sent to your wallet. This should be done in several minutes in most cases. During peak periods, it could take longer.

Which PumpkinCat is the rarest?
We will publish a table with all traits and their rarity before the launch.

What wallet can I use?
Only use a Cardano Shelley-era wallet like Yoroi, Daedalus, Adalite or Nami. Do not use a wallet from an exchange, your NFT will be lost forever.

What happens if I send the wrong amount?
A refund will arrive in your wallet for the amount with the transaction fee subtracted. To avoid this, you need to send the exact amount.

Are there any limits?
You will be able to mint 3 NFTs at once. Just send a multiple of 18 (36 for 2, 54 for 3). Make sure to send the exact amount. Sending more funds will initiate a refund.

Why isn't my refund the full amount?
We have to subtract the transaction fee (approximately 0.18 - 0.20 ADA) when we are processing the refund.
