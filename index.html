About

There are more than a million possible PumpkinCatz variations, with just the cutest 500 PumpkinCatz in the first drop. The first 500 PumpkinCatz are already sold out, they were available to mint for 18 ADA. Every single PumpkinCat can only be minted once, some will happen to have very rare traits – like the pumpkin border.
Our ambition is to build an active community across all relevant CNFT platforms and bring attention to the Cardano Ecosystem.
But also to do something good - 10 % of the revenue collected will be donated to help shelterless cats.

Donations

From the very beginning when we got the idea of doing a NFT project in honor of our beloved cat Pumpkin we were convinced that we have to use some of the raised funds to donate to charities which care about shelterless cats (and pets). We announced that 10 % of our revenue will be allocated to a donation wallet - this would have been 700 and a little ₳DA, we decided to top it up and put 1000 ₳DA into our donation wallet.
The first charity we choose to donate to is: Jardinet dels Gats, an organization aiming to help shelterless cats in the city center of Barcelona. We have already visited the site a few years ago and were impressed by the amazing job they are doing!

FAQ

When will I be able to mint PumpkinCatz NFT?
Monday, Sept. 13th, 20.00 UTC

What's the initial mint price?
18 ₳DA

When will I get my PumpkinCat?
After you send the funds to the wallet, it will be minted and sent to your wallet. This should be done in several minutes in most cases. During peak periods, it could take longer.

Which PumpkinCat is the rarest?
We will publish a table with all traits and their rarity before the launch.

What wallet can I use?
Only use a Cardano Shelley-era wallet like Yoroi, Daedalus, Adalite or Nami. Do not use a wallet from an exchange, your NFT will be lost forever.

What happens if I send the wrong amount?
A refund will arrive in your wallet for the amount with the transaction fee subtracted. To avoid this, you need to send the exact amount.

Are there any limits?
You will be able to mint 5 NFTs at once. Just send a multiple of 18 (36 for 2, 54 for 3, and so on). Make sure to send the exact amount. Sending more funds will initiate a refund.

Why isn't my refund the full amount?
We have to subtract the transaction fee (approximately 0.18 - 0.20 ADA) when we are processing the refund.
